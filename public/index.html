The Key Features of our Job

Bus Location Support

Students can see their desired bus location and also they can see all the bus locations.

Finding study materials

Students can find their previous notes, lectures and other study materials with a few clicks.

Crowd Sourcing

Students can share their location if they are inside their regular bus.

Our buses

AUST Junction is an easy-to-use website that enables students to see the location of bus on a smartphone, tablet, or personal computer.
